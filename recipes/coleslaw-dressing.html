Coleslaw Dressing

Description

This is a creamy coleslaw dressing that can be made with ingredients that you likely already have at home. This dressing requires minimal ingredients and prep work and will transform shredded cabbage and carrots into a refreshing, creamy side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Store in an airtight container.
